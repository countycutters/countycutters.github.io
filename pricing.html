Pricing FAQs

Are there any hidden costs?

No, our pricing is completely transparent. The price you see is what you pay. We'll always discuss any additional work with you before proceeding.

Can I cancel or pause my service?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduling. You can pause your service during holidays or cancel with one week's notice. We understand that circumstances change.

Do you offer discounts for long-term contracts?

Yes! We offer special rates for customers who commit to 6-month or 12-month contracts. Contact us to discuss your options.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfer, and all major credit/debit cards. Payment is due after each service visit or can be set up as a monthly direct debit.
